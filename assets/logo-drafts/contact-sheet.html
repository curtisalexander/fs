Failed Star — logo drafts

A cartoon five-point star that is hunched over, disappointed it never ignited (a "failed star" is a brown dwarf). Three takes below, each shown at 128 / 48 / 24 px on the project's dark (#0e0d12) and light (#f5f1e8) themes. The 24 px column is the favicon stress test.

1 · Flat mono mark

Minimal single-color silhouette; the face is punched out of the body so the whole thing is one shape and can inherit a CSS color. The star leans right and bows its head left. Built to survive heavy scaling.

3 · Brown dwarf

Astronomical mood: a dim star inside a faint heat glow, with tiny orbiting motes and a cold spark that fizzled above its head. Dusty ember-to-red-magenta palette. Best at large sizes; the glow softens it as a favicon (see 24 px).
