Bite-sized notes on things we figured out along the way — the reasoning behind a decision, and background worth keeping. Each one cross-links the three sources (the book, ds4, Raschka). They complement the milestone writeups and the big-picture map.

Markdown is the source of truth. These pages are hand-distilled from the docs/learnings/*.md notes — same content, with the ASCII diagrams promoted to real SVG. tools/sync-check.sh flags when a page drifts from its source.
